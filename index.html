Bayesian Statistics & Machine Learning

I do research in statistics with the focus on causality, MCMC methods and modeling. My job focuses on machine learning in marketing and teaching professionals and students to understand and enjoy data. Oh, and I also write sometimes.

Research

My focus on my alma mater focuses on bayesian causality through Direct acyclic graphs (DAGs), Bayesian networks and state-space models. Secondary goal is the popularizatoin of bayesian techniques which can offer significant advantages compared to standard frequentistic methods, such as the null hypothesis significance (NHST) framework.

Teaching

I have extensive experience teaching data science, statistics, and programming at university level and in professional workshops.
